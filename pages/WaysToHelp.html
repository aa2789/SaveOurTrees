Plant Trees

Reforestation is a vital part of the climate change strategy and provides important habitat for endangered species by restoring previously degraded habitats. If you would like to combat deforestation, plant trees or contribute to some of the following organizations that plant trees.

  • One Tree Planted
  • Arbor Day Foundation
  • Trees for the Future
  • American Forests
